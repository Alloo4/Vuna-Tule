Overview

Vuna Tule is an innovative mobile application that will revolutionize the agricultural market by directly connecting farmers with consumers. The primary functionalities of the application include enabling farmers to list their produce, facilitating direct negotiations on prices and quantities, and integrating transportation logistics to streamline the delivery process.

Problem Statement

  • Most farmers from a common geographical area produce the same types of commodities. This saturation forces them to sell at very low prices causing them to incur losses.
  • The market relies heavily on intermediaries who add layers of cost and complexity, often resulting in farmers receiving minimal profits for their hard work.
